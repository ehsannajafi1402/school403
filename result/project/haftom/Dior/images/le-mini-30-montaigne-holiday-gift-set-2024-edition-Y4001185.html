Le Mini 30 Montaigne Holiday Gift Set 2024 Edition

Discovery Set with 4 Miniatures
In Stock

description

The House of Dior unveils a discovery set that depicts the facade of the historic boutique at 30 Avenue Montaigne. A miniature version of the Dior Beauty advent calendar, Le Mini 30 Montaigne opens to reveal a sumptuous golden design created by artist Pietro Ruffo and inspired by French-style gardens.

This Dior gift set includes 4 miniature Dior makeup, skincare and fragrance icons: 2 Dior perfumes with floral and fresh notes Miss Dior and J'adore, a Prestige La Micro-Huile de Rose serum, and a Rouge Dior lipstick in shade 999, velvet finish.

Le Mini 30 Montaigne case is reusable and makes a stunning storage box.

Dimensions of the set once opened: 7.9" H x 6.3" W x 2.7"
Product volumes and weights:
J'adore Eau de Parfum .17 oz
Miss Dior Blooming Bouquet .17 oz
Dior Prestige La Micro-Huile de Rose Activated Serum .34 oz
Rouge Dior lipstick .05 oz
