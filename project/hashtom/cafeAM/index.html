What is coffee? ☕

Coffee is a common drink that is obtained from roasted and ground coffee beans. Coffee is slightly acidic and because of its high caffeine content, it is a stimulant. This drink is the most popular hot drink in the world. The coffee plant is native to Yemen and Africa, but much more than these areas, it is cultivated in the subtropical and tropical regions of the American continent and Southeast Asia. It is also relatively less cultivated in some areas of South Asia such as India. When the fruit of the coffee plant ripens, the coffee beans are harvested and processed. they do. The first Starbucks coffee shop was opened on March 31, 1971 in Seattle by three friends, an English teacher named Jerry Baldwin, a history teacher named Zoe Siegel, and a writer named Gordon Booker, who met at the University of San Francisco. started himself The motivation of these three people in setting up the above place where coffee beans and various related equipment were sold was a person named Alfred Peet who taught them how to roast coffee. This store located in Pike Place neighborhood is still active. In 1982, the original founder of this company, Howard Schultz, joined the partners with the offer to sell espresso and coffee. At that time, most people believed that coffee should only be prepared in people's homes; Therefore, Schultz's proposal was not accepted by the other three partners. According to them, entering the field of selling drinks would cause a deviation and prevent them from reaching their desired goal. In 1985, Schultz alone founded a chain of coffee shops under the name "Il Journale". In 1987, the owners of Starbucks handed over the franchise of their stores to Schultz. Schultz quickly merged these two collections and started expanding Starbucks coffee shops in different countries. The first Starbucks chain coffee shops were established in the same year in the cities of Chicago in America and Vancouver in Canada.
